UNIV. OF KENTUCKY · EGR 215 · TEAM OF 4

Automated Water Sampler — Rotating Base

Team "Built On Truss" · My subsystem: the rotating base

A four-person system that lowers a pipette, collects a 1 mL water sample, and dispenses it into cups indexed by a rotating base, with a vision system measuring pipette-to-sludge distance. I designed and built the gear-driven rotating base — a stepper-driven, laser-cut turntable that indexes by a fixed rotation under MATLAB control.

CAD in Onshape with dimensioned drawings released for fabrication. Integrating four independently designed subsystems surfaced real interface issues — stand clearance over the cups, end-to-end stepper automation — early lessons in interface management I later applied at production scale.
